Account How do I register as a vendor?

To register as a vendor in our portal, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our vendor registration page at https://vendor.example.com/register
  2. Complete the registration form with your company details
  3. Upload required documents (business license, W-9 or tax documents, insurance certificates if applicable)
  4. Create your login credentials
  5. Verify your email address through the confirmation link

After submission, our team will review your application within 3-5 business days and notify you of approval via email.

Need help with registration? Contact our vendor support team at vendor-support@example.com

Account How do I update my banking details?

To update your banking information in the vendor portal:

  1. Log in to your vendor account
  2. Navigate to "Profile" > "Payment Information" (or "Banking Details")
  3. Click on "Edit Banking Details"
  4. Enter your new banking information
  5. Verify the details carefully to avoid payment delays
  6. Submit for review

Please note that banking changes require verification and may take 3-5 business days to process. During this time, any scheduled payments might be delayed.

For security reasons, you cannot update banking information if you have pending payments scheduled within the next 48 hours.

Invoicing Where can I see the status of my submitted invoice?

You can track the status of your invoices in the 'Invoices' section of the portal. Here's how:

  1. Log in to your vendor account
  2. Click on "Invoices" in the main navigation menu
  3. View the "Status" column for each invoice

Your invoice will display one of the following statuses:

Submitted

Invoice has been received but not yet processed

Pending Approval

Under review by our finance team

Approved

Verified and scheduled for payment

Rejected

Denied with reason (click for details)

Paid

Payment has been processed

You can also set up email notifications for invoice status changes.

Configure this in your "Account Settings" > "Notifications" tab.

Invoicing How do I correct errors on a rejected invoice?

If your invoice has been rejected, follow these steps to correct and resubmit it:

  1. Navigate to "Invoices" in the portal
  2. Filter by "Rejected" status or locate the invoice in the list
  3. Click on the invoice number to view details
  4. Review the rejection reason provided in the "Feedback" section
  5. Click "Edit Invoice" to make necessary corrections
  6. Update the information as needed based on the feedback
  7. Click "Resubmit Invoice" when finished

Note: Resubmitted invoices will go through the approval process again. The original submission date will be preserved for payment term calculations.
