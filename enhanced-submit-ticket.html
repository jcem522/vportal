Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to get a response?

Our support team typically responds within 24-48 hours during business days.

Can I update my ticket after submission?

Yes, you can add comments or attachments to your ticket by replying to the confirmation email.

What if my issue is urgent?

For critical issues affecting your business operations, select "High" priority and consider using our Live Chat during business hours.
