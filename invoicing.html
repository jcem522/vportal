Invoicing FAQs

How do I link invoices to milestones?

When creating a new invoice, select the relevant PO first, then choose the appropriate milestone from the dropdown menu. You can only invoice against completed and approved milestones.

Why was my invoice rejected?

Common reasons include: missing delivery documentation, incorrect milestone reference, amount discrepancy with PO, or missing supporting documents. Check the comments on the rejected invoice for specific details.

How long does invoice approval take?

Standard approval time is 3-5 business days from submission, assuming all documentation is correct. Payment terms begin once an invoice is approved.
